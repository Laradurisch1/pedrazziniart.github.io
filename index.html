Biography

Lara Pedrazzini

Lara Pedrazzini was born in 1993 in Zurich, Switzerland — a city that shaped her sensitivity and her eye for beauty from an early age. After completing her schooling in Zurich, she followed a path that led her through the worlds of sales and early childhood education, gathering along the way a deep understanding of people and human connection.

In 2016, a turning point arrived: Lara took on the management of a small gallery in Seefeld, Zurich. Though the gallery no longer exists, the experience proved formative — igniting in her a desire to no longer simply display art, but to create it.

Supported by the love and generosity of her grandparents and her mother — herself a celebrated artist in Zurich — Lara found the courage to begin her own artistic journey. The legacy of creativity she inherited became both her foundation and her freedom.

Her work is born from lived experience. Past events, emotions, and moments of vulnerability are transformed on the canvas into something luminous and expressive. Lara's art does not shy away from darkness — instead, it gently invites the viewer to encounter their own feelings, to sit with them, and ultimately, to heal.

To feel is to be human. Lara Pedrazzini makes that feeling visible.
